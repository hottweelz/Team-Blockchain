Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about blockchain, cryptocurrency, Web3, and Digital Bytes.

What is blockchain technology?

Blockchain is a decentralized digital ledger technology that records transactions across multiple computers, ensuring transparency, security, and immutability. It forms the foundation for cryptocurrencies, smart contracts, and distributed applications across industries.

What is cryptocurrency and how does it work?

Cryptocurrency is a digital currency secured by cryptography and operates on blockchain networks. Unlike traditional currencies controlled by governments, cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin and Ethereum are decentralized, enabling peer-to-peer transactions without intermediaries.

What is Web3 and DeFi?

Web3 refers to the next generation of the internet built on decentralized technologies like blockchain. DeFi (Decentralized Finance) enables financial services—lending, trading, yield farming—without traditional banks, using smart contracts on blockchain networks.

What is Digital Bytes by TeamBlockchain?

Digital Bytes is a weekly blockchain and digital asset insights platform providing comprehensive analysis, news, and expert commentary through our newsletter and podcast. We cover blockchain developments, market analysis, regulatory updates, and industry trends.

How can blockchain improve my business?

Blockchain can enhance supply chain transparency, reduce transaction costs, enable smart contract automation, improve data security, and create new revenue streams through tokenization. Our newsletter and podcast explore real-world enterprise blockchain applications.
