# Enterprise Blockchain: Real-World Adoption

**Episode 20 | 49 minutes | Published February 28, 2026**

## Overview

Enterprise blockchain is moving beyond hype into measurable ROI. This episode features real-world case studies from supply chain, trade finance, and asset tokenization. Learn what works, what doesn't, and the path to scale.

## Key Topics

- **Supply Chain Tracking:** Provenance and transparency at scale
- **Trade Finance:** Digital settlement and letter of credit automation
- **Asset Tokenization:** Securities, real estate, and commodities on blockchain
- **Enterprise Architecture:** Integration with legacy systems
- **Regulatory Compliance:** Enterprise frameworks for privacy and auditability
- **ROI Measurement:** How enterprises quantify blockchain value
- **Lessons Learned:** Common pitfalls and success patterns
