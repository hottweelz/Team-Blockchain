# Bitcoin's Role in Modern Finance

**Episode 24 | 52 minutes | Published March 28, 2026**

## Overview

Bitcoin continues to evolve from speculative asset to institutional hedge instrument. In this episode, James Tylee and guests discuss Bitcoin's role in modern portfolio construction, adoption trends among institutional investors, and the macro factors driving 2026 price dynamics.

## Key Topics

- **Institutional Adoption:** How pension funds and hedge funds integrate Bitcoin
- **Portfolio Diversification:** Bitcoin as non-correlated asset class
- **Macro Environment:** Interest rates, inflation, and Bitcoin valuation
- **Market Structure:** Spot ETFs, futures, and custody solutions
- **Regulatory Tailwinds:** Global acceptance and compliance frameworks
