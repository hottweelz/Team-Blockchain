# DeFi Risks and Opportunities in 2026

**Episode 22 | 55 minutes | Published March 14, 2026**

## Overview

DeFi has matured with $50B+ in total value locked, but risks persist. This episode explores smart contract security, liquidity fragmentation, and the emerging opportunities in structured products and risk-adjusted yield strategies.

## Key Topics

- **Protocol Security:** Audit standards and risk assessment
- **Liquidity Landscape:** Concentrated liquidity (Uniswap V4) and AMM competition
- **Yield Strategies:** Farming, staking, and options trading in DeFi
- **Systemic Risk:** Interconnected protocol risks and contagion
- **Institutional Entry:** How professional investors evaluate DeFi
- **Emerging Tokens:** New DeFi protocols gaining traction
