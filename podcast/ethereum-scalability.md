# Ethereum Scalability Solutions Explained

**Episode 23 | 48 minutes | Published March 21, 2026**

## Overview

Ethereum Layer 2 solutions have matured dramatically. This episode breaks down rollup technology, discusses competitive dynamics between Arbitrum and Optimism, and explores the implications for DeFi and enterprise adoption.

## Key Topics

- **Layer 2 Fundamentals:** How rollups work and why they matter
- **Arbitrum vs. Optimism:** Market share and technical differences
- **DeFi Migration:** Which protocols are moving to Layer 2
- **Cost Impact:** Transaction fees across chain layers
- **Enterprise Applications:** Scalability meets enterprise requirements
- **Future Roadmap:** Ethereum 2.0 implications and Layer 3
