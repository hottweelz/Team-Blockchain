# Digital Bytes Product

## Overview

**Digital Bytes** is a weekly blockchain and digital asset insights platform combining a professional newsletter and expert-hosted podcast. We deliver comprehensive analysis, regulatory updates, and market trends to thousands of professionals in the blockchain ecosystem.

## Product Details

### Distribution Channels

**Newsletter (Substack)**
- Published: Every week
- Format: In-depth written analysis
- Subscribers: 5,000+ active readers
- URL: https://digitalbytes.substack.com/
- Content: Blockchain developments, DeFi analysis, regulatory updates, market insights

**Podcast (Spotify & Cyber.FM)**
- Published: Weekly episodes
- Format: Expert discussion and interviews
- Runtime: 45-60 minutes per episode
- Hosts: Jonny Fry (CEO), James Tylee (Podcast Host)
- Platforms: Spotify, Cyber.FM radio
- Topics: Blockchain technology, cryptocurrency markets, DeFi, Web3 innovations
