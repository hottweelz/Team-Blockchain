US State Privacy Rights

Plain Language Summary: This page explains your privacy rights under US state laws, including California, Colorado, Virginia, Connecticut, Utah, Texas, Oregon, and others. We are committed to protecting your data and making it easy for you to exercise your rights.

Last updated: October 3, 2025

Accessibility Commitment

TeamBlockchain is committed to providing an accessible experience for all users, including those using assistive technologies. If you encounter any accessibility barriers, please contact us at info@teamblockchain.net.

1. Your Privacy Rights

  • Right to Know: You can request information about the personal data we collect, use, disclose, or sell.
  • Right to Access: You can request a copy of your personal data.
  • Right to Delete: You can request deletion of your personal data, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to Correct: You can request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to Opt Out: You can opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal data and targeted advertising.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: You will not be discriminated against for exercising your privacy rights.

2. Categories of Data We Collect

  • Identifiers (e.g., name, email address, account ID)
  • Usage data (e.g., analytics, device/browser info)
  • API keys and credentials (encrypted)
  • Any information you provide when contacting support

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data as necessary to provide our services or as required by law.

3. California Residents - CCPA Notice

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) provides you with specific rights regarding your personal information. This section supplements our Privacy Policy with information specifically for California consumers.

Your California Privacy Rights Include:

  • Right to Know: You have the right to request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you, the categories of sources, our business purpose for collecting or selling personal information, and the categories of third parties with whom we share personal information.
  • Right to Delete: You have the right to request deletion of personal information we collected from you, subject to certain exceptions under CCPA.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale: You have the right to opt-out of the "sale" of your personal information. We do not sell your personal information.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: You have the right not to receive discriminatory treatment for exercising your CCPA privacy rights.
  • Right to Correct: You have the right to request correction of inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Personal Information: You have the right to limit our use of your sensitive personal information, if applicable.

Do Not Sell My Personal Information

TeamBlockchain does not sell personal information as defined by CCPA. We do not share your personal information with third parties for monetary or other valuable consideration. If our practices change, we will update this notice and provide you with an opportunity to opt-out of such sale.

Authorized Agent

California residents may designate an authorized agent to make CCPA requests on their behalf. To designate an authorized agent, please provide written authorization signed by you and the agent, or a valid power of attorney. We may require verification of both your and the agent's identity before processing the request.

CCPA Data Collection Disclosure (Past 12 Months)

In the past 12 months, we have collected the following categories of personal information: Identifiers (email, name, account ID), Internet/Network Activity (usage data, analytics), and Professional Information (if provided in communications). We collect this information directly from you, automatically through your use of our services, and from third-party service providers (e.g., analytics providers).

Business Purpose: We use this information to provide and improve our services, communicate with you, ensure security, comply with legal obligations, and analyze usage patterns.

Sharing: We share personal information with service providers (e.g., email services, analytics providers) who assist us in operating our platform. We do not share or sell personal information for marketing purposes.

4. How to Exercise Your Rights

You can exercise your privacy rights by contacting us at info@teamblockchain.net. Please include your request and sufficient information for us to verify your identity. We will respond within the timeframes required by law.

5. Notice of Changes

We may update this page as new state laws are enacted or existing laws change. Material changes will be communicated via email or platform notification where required.

6. Contact Information

If you have questions or requests regarding your privacy rights, contact us at info@teamblockchain.net.
