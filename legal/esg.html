Plain Language Summary: This page outlines TeamBlockchain' (TeamBlockchain) commitment to Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) principles, including accessibility. We believe responsible innovation and ethical practices are essential to our mission and long-term success.

Last updated: October 3, 2025

Accessibility Commitment

TeamBlockchain is committed to making our platform accessible to all users, including those with disabilities. Our approach is guided by the following principles:

  • Screen Reader Support: TeamBlockchain is designed to work with industry-standard screen readers (e.g., VoiceOver, NVDA, JAWS) to ensure users with visual impairments can navigate and interact with our platform.
  • Color Vision Deficiency (CVD) Support: We recognize the importance of color in financial analytics. We are working to provide alternative color schemes and high-contrast modes to support users with color vision deficiencies.
  • Accessibility Standards: Our goal is to meet or exceed the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 AA and Section 508 of the Rehabilitation Act.
  • Mobile Accessibility: Our mobile and PWA experiences are designed to support platform accessibility features, including VoiceOver (iOS) and TalkBack (Android).
  • Continuous Improvement: We conduct ongoing research and user testing, and we welcome feedback from users with disabilities to help us improve.
  • Transparency: We are committed to publishing information about our accessibility features and limitations, and to providing updates as we make progress.
  • Contact for Assistance: If you encounter any accessibility barriers or need assistance, please contact us at info@teamblockchain.net.

While we strive for full accessibility, we acknowledge that some features may not yet be fully accessible. We are dedicated to continuous improvement and to making TeamBlockchain inclusive for all users.

Environmental

  • Energy Efficiency & Responsible Hosting: TeamBlockchain’s primary server infrastructure is hosted in the Equinix Datacenter in Amsterdam, a facility renowned for its commitment to sustainability and responsible digital infrastructure. Equinix’s Future First strategy focuses on delivering secure, efficient, and low-carbon solutions that help customers achieve their own environmental goals.
  • Renewable Energy: We prioritize data centers and cloud providers with strong renewable energy and sustainability programs. Equinix’s approach supports the global transition to a low-carbon economy.
  • Carbon Footprint: We are committed to measuring and reducing our carbon footprint. We conduct annual audits of our server energy usage and set goals to reduce emissions year over year.
  • Sustainable Procurement: We prefer to work with vendors and partners who share our commitment to environmental responsibility.
  • Digital Sustainability: We minimize unnecessary data storage and processing, and encourage efficient code and resource usage throughout our development process.
  • Transparency: We will publish periodic updates on our environmental impact and progress toward our sustainability goals.
  • Community Engagement: We support and participate in industry initiatives aimed at improving the sustainability of digital infrastructure.

Social

  • Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion: TeamBlockchain is founded and led by individuals with lived experience of disability (our founder lives with Osteogenesis Imperfecta Type 4 and uses a wheelchair). We are committed to building a diverse, inclusive, and equitable team and user community. We provide equal opportunities and do not tolerate discrimination of any kind.
  • Accessibility: Our personal experience drives our commitment to making TeamBlockchain usable by everyone, regardless of ability. We welcome feedback to help us improve accessibility for all users.
  • User Empowerment & Education: We provide clear documentation, responsive support, and educational resources to help users understand and benefit from our platform.
  • Community Engagement: We support open-source, educational, and community-driven projects in fintech, crypto, and AI. We participate in industry events and encourage knowledge sharing.
  • Ethical Technology: We are committed to the responsible and ethical use of AI and data, with transparency and user control at the core of our product design.
  • User Privacy and Safety: We prioritize user privacy and data security, and empower users to control their information.
  • Feedback and Grievance: We welcome feedback and provide clear channels for users and stakeholders to raise concerns or suggestions.

Governance

  • Community and Industry Engagement: We actively participate in external DAOs (Decentralized Autonomous Organizations) and industry groups, including those focused on ESG, finance, and blockchain. This ensures we are exposed to diverse viewpoints, stay aligned with evolving regulations, and collaborate with like-minded organizations.
  • Ethical Leadership and Morals: We believe in leading with integrity, transparency, and a strong moral compass, even in rapidly evolving fields like finance and blockchain.
  • Transparency: We are open about our governance practices, decision-making processes, and use of technology, and we regularly publish updates for stakeholders.
  • Data Privacy, Security, and Compliance: We uphold the highest standards of data privacy, security, and regulatory compliance, and review our policies annually or as regulations evolve.
  • Responsible AI: We are transparent about our use of AI/ML and provide clear information about automated decision-making, with user control at the core of our product design.
  • Continuous Improvement: We welcome feedback and collaboration to improve our governance and adapt to new challenges and opportunities.
