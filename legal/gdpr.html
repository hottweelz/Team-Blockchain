GDPR Information

Plain Language Summary: This page explains your rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) if you are located in the European Union or United Kingdom. We are committed to protecting your data and supporting your rights.

Last updated: October 3, 2025

Accessibility Commitment

TeamBlockchain is committed to providing an accessible experience for all users, including those using assistive technologies. If you encounter any accessibility barriers, please contact us at info@teamblockchain.net.

1. What is GDPR?

The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) is a European law that gives individuals rights over their personal data and sets requirements for organizations that process such data. TeamBlockchain operates servers in both the European Union and the United States, and complies with GDPR for all EU/EEA/UK users.

2. Lawful Basis for Processing

  • We process your data to provide and improve our services (contractual necessity)
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • With your consent (where required)
  • For our legitimate interests (e.g., security, analytics), balanced with your rights

3. International Data Transfers

Your personal data may be transferred between the EU and other countries, including the United States. We use appropriate safeguards, such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs), to protect your data during such transfers.

4. Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach affecting EU/EEA/UK residents, TeamBlockchain will notify the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours, and affected individuals without undue delay, as required by GDPR.

5. Your Rights Under GDPR

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data (“right to be forgotten”)
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests or direct marketing
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time (where processing is based on consent)
  • Complaint: Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

6. How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise your GDPR rights, contact us at info@teamblockchain.net. Please include your request and sufficient information for us to verify your identity. We will respond within the timeframes required by law.

Contact Information

If you have questions or requests regarding GDPR or your data protection rights, contact us at info@teamblockchain.net. If a Data Protection Officer (DPO) is appointed, their contact information will be listed here.
