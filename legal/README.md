# Legal Documents - TeamBlockchain

This folder contains all legal documentation for TeamBlockchain and the Digital Bytes newsletter and podcast.

## Files

1. **legal.html** - Legal disclaimer and general legal information
2. **privacy.html** - Privacy Policy detailing data collection and usage
3. **tos.html** - Terms of Service for using TeamBlockchain content
4. **cookies.html** - Cookies Policy explaining cookie usage
5. **gdpr.html** - GDPR compliance information for EU users
6. **us-privacy.html** - US State Privacy Rights (CCPA and other state laws)
7. **esg.html** - ESG (Environmental, Social, Governance) statement
