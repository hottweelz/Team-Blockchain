Cookies Policy

Plain Language Summary: This page explains what cookies and similar technologies TeamBlockchain uses, why we use them, and how you can manage your preferences. If you have questions, contact info@teamblockchain.net.

Last updated: October 3, 2025

Accessibility Commitment

TeamBlockchain is committed to providing an accessible experience for all users, including those using assistive technologies. If you encounter any accessibility barriers, please contact us at info@teamblockchain.net.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, login status, and other information to improve your experience.

2. What Cookies and Technologies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the basic operation of TeamBlockchain (e.g., session management, security).
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with TeamBlockchain so we can improve our services (e.g., Google Analytics, if used).
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences (e.g., language, theme).
  • Third-Party Cookies: Set by services we integrate with (e.g., embedded content, APIs). These are subject to the third party’s policies.

We do not use cookies to sell your data or for targeted advertising.

3. How to Manage Cookies

  • You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings.
  • Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies, or to set preferences for certain sites.
  • Disabling some cookies may affect the functionality of TeamBlockchain.

For more information, see your browser’s help documentation.

4. Contact Information

If you have questions about our use of cookies or your choices, contact us at info@teamblockchain.net.
