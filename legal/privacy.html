Privacy Policy

This policy applies to our web application, Progressive Web App (PWA), and any future mobile applications.

Accessibility Commitment

TeamBlockchain is committed to providing an accessible experience for all users, including those using assistive technologies. If you encounter any accessibility barriers, please contact us at info@teamblockchain.net.

1. What Data We Collect

  • Identifiers (e.g., name, email address, account ID)
  • Usage data (e.g., analytics, device/browser info, IP address)
  • API keys and credentials (encrypted)
  • Support communications
  • Cookies and similar technologies (see Cookies Policy)

2. Where We Store and Process Data

TeamBlockchain stores and processes your data on secure servers located in the United States and the European Union. Your data may be processed in either location depending on your region and the services you use.

3. International Data Transfers

Your personal data may be transferred to, and processed in, countries other than your own, including the United States and the European Union. We use appropriate safeguards, such as Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission, to protect your data when it is transferred internationally.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve our services
  • To secure your account and our platform
  • To communicate with you (support, updates, notifications)
  • To analyze usage and improve user experience
  • To comply with legal obligations

5. How We Share Your Data

  • With service providers (e.g., hosting, analytics, email) as needed to operate TeamBlockchain
  • With third-party APIs/exchanges you connect (subject to their terms)
  • As required by law or to protect rights and safety
  • We do not sell your personal data

4. Data Security

  • All sensitive data (API keys, credentials) is encrypted at rest and in transit
  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data
  • No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security

5. Data Retention

TeamBlockchain retains your personal data only as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. When your data is no longer needed, it is deleted or anonymized, subject to applicable legal requirements.

6. Your Rights

  • US residents: See US State Privacy Rights for your state-specific rights
  • EU/UK residents: See GDPR for your data protection rights
  • All users: Contact us at info@teamblockchain.net to access, correct, or delete your data

Children’s Privacy

TeamBlockchain is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 18. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact info@teamblockchain.net and we will promptly remove the information.

6. Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies and similar technologies to analyze usage and improve our services. For details and your choices, see our Cookies Policy.

Automated Decision-Making

TeamBlockchain may use artificial intelligence and machine learning models to generate analysis and recommendations. However, TeamBlockchain does not make fully automated decisions that have legal or similarly significant effects on users. If you have questions or concerns about automated processing, contact info@teamblockchain.net.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email or platform notification where required. Continued use of TeamBlockchain after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

8. Contact Information

If you have questions or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, contact us at info@teamblockchain.net.
