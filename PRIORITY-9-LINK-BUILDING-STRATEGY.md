## 📰 Phase 3: HARO & Journalist Outreach (Ongoing)

**Platform:** Register at helpareporter.com  
**Frequency:** 1-2 expert responses daily to relevant queries  

**Keywords to Watch:**
- "blockchain regulation"
- "crypto compliance"
- "Web3 policy"
- "digital assets"
- "fintech innovation"
- "ESG blockchain"
- "DeFi risks"
- "cryptocurrency AML"

**Response Template:**
```
Subject: Expert Response: [Original Query] – Crypto/Blockchain Perspective

Hi [Reporter Name],

I'm James Tylee from Team Blockchain, and I work extensively with blockchain companies 
on regulatory compliance and digital asset strategy. Regarding your query on [TOPIC], 
I can provide:

[2-3 specific, quotable insights relevant to their question]

Here's additional context: [Link to relevant Digital Bytes article/podcast]

Would you be available for a brief call to discuss further? I can provide more details 
on [angle they might miss].

Best regards,
James Tylee
Team Blockchain
```

**Success Rate Expectation:** 1 in 10-15 responses → media mention with link  
**Annual Projection:** 24-36 media mentions @ 50% with links = 12-18 high-authority backlinks

## 💡 Pro Tips for Success

1. **Quality Over Quantity:** 1 link from CoinDesk > 10 links from low-authority blogs
2. **Relationship First:** Build genuine relationships with journalists/editors before asking for links
3. **Newsjacking:** Monitor crypto regulatory news, respond quickly with expert commentary
4. **Updated Assets:** Keep compliance guides updated monthly → "Updated for March 2026" drives re-linking
5. **Testimonials:** Request testimonials from link partners → use social proof in outreach
6. **Monthly Outreach Quota:** Set minimum of 10-15 outreaches per week
7. **Email Personalization:** 50%+ open rates come from personalized subject lines
8. **Follow-up Sequence:** Initial pitch → Wait 7 days → Follow-up → Wait 7 days → Final follow-up
9. **Track Response Rates:** Monitor which angles generate best responses
10. **Celebrate Wins:** Share new backlinks internally → keeps team motivated
